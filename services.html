Services

Phirun Vannak & Partners is a law firm dedicated to providing a broad range of professional legal services that reflects our commitment to integrity and honesty and our thorough knowledge of the law. We have experienced lawyers at Phirun Vannak & Partners delivering professional legal services to our clients both locally and internationally in the areas not limited to the following services:

ESTATE PLANNING

If you are considering putting an estate plan together to specify the distribution of your property upon death, or if you have been named as executor or administrator of the estate of a loved one, you want an experienced attorney to guide you through the process. The estate planning process involves a wide range of tools to help you meet your goals, and the probate process can be confusing and intimidating. At the law office of Phirun Vannak & Partners, we bring a wealth of experience to clients who need to resolve estate or probate issues. In developing personalized estate plans that meet our client's specific needs, we take a straight forward approach, carefully explaining your options, as well as the potential consequences of different strategies. We draft and review essential documents, including wills, power of attorney, health care directives and so on.

CORPORATE GOVERNANCE

Additionally, we assist our clients in the day-to-day operation of their businesses. More than creating businesses and providing counsel on real estate transactions, we are available to provide legal advice on a full range of legal matters that affect your business. From when to conduct meetings, to how to record corporate minutes, to maintaining corporate records, to voting on issues or employment law matters, we have the capacity to assist you as needed. Our commercial and real estate transaction practice assists a range of clients. We tailor our approach to the matter at hand. Using years of experience to identify & address relevant issues regardless of the size of the transaction or areas of law involved. Over the years, this approach has helped us effectively handle all types of transactions.

REPRESENTATION

We take a straightforward approach, providing you with a thorough assessment of the strengths and weaknesses of your case, as well as your options and likelihood of success.
